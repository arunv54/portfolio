Projects



CFD Analysis of H2 mixture distribution in HDI Engine

This project involves CFD simulations in CONVERGE to study hydrogen direct injection in HDI engines with high tumble configuration, focusing on turbulence modeling validated against PLIF data, with post-processing and flow analysis in Paraview and Python.


  • Find out more

Design and Development of a Novel Surface-cum-Underwater Robotic System

This project involves designing and simulating a SurfUR prototype capable of surface navigation and vertical dive-in/dive-out using a variable mass/volume buoyancy mechanism. It includes the development of various subsystems, supported by CFD simulations to evaluate hydrodynamic performance.


  • Detailed Report
  • Find out more

Design of Portable Telescopic Agitator for Industrial Slurry Homogenization

This project involves designing a portable telescopic agitator (1–5 m extension) in SolidWorks for industrial slurry cleaning as part of the Inter IIT Solinas Product Design Challenge. It integrates trapezoidal threaded shafts with stepper motor–driven telescopic motion and waterproof rubber bellows, with performance validated through ANSYS structural and CFD simulations.
